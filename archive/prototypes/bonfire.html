About the Event

A Night to Remember

Every November, hundreds of Darrington residents come together on the Village Field for one of the most cherished events in the village calendar — a roaring community bonfire followed by a breathtaking professional fireworks display.

Organised entirely by volunteers from the Darrington Village Field Trust, the event has grown into a spectacular annual tradition that draws visitors from across the surrounding area.

Whether you're watching the bonfire light up, listening to the crackle of the flames, or craning your neck at the fireworks above — it's a night that brings the whole community together.

Practical Information

Getting There

  • Darrington Village Field, Park Avenue, Darrington, WF8
  • Parking is available on surrounding roads — please be considerate of residents
  • A short walk from the village centre

Safety

  • A safety perimeter is in place around the bonfire and fireworks area
  • Please follow marshals' instructions at all times
  • No personal fireworks or sparklers permitted on site
  • Dogs on leads welcome

What to Bring

  • Warm clothing — November nights can be cold
  • Waterproof footwear recommended
  • Cash for stalls and food (card may be available)
  • Ear defenders for young children if needed
