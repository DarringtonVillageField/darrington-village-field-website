About the Field

A Field with a Story

Darrington Village Field has been a treasured part of the village for generations. Maintained by the Darrington Village Field Trust — a small group of dedicated volunteers — the field exists entirely for the benefit of the community.

From toddlers on the play equipment to families watching the fireworks, from dog walkers enjoying the open green to runners pounding the course on Feast & Fayre day, the field belongs to everyone in Darrington.

The Field

Something for Everyone

The Village Field is open every day, free of charge. Here's what you'll find.

🧒

Children's Play Area

New equipment installed in 2024, including a climbing tower, big slide, balance swing and much more — for all ages from toddlers upwards.

⚽

Football Pitch

A full-size open grass area with goals — perfect for informal kickabouts, sports days and the 5 Mile Run finishing straight.

🏋️

Outdoor Gym

Free outdoor exercise equipment and a basketball court — for older children, teenagers and adults to keep active.

🌳

Open Green Space

Wide open lawns, mature trees, benches and the Jubilee tree — ideal for picnics, dog walking, and simply being outside.

Darrington Village Field Trust is a volunteer-run community organisation dedicated to maintaining and improving the Village Field for all residents of Darrington.
