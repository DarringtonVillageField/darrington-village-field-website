Governance

Responsible care for a community asset

Darrington Village Field Trust is accountable for protecting, maintaining and improving the Village Field for public benefit.

Charity identity

Darrington Village Field Trust

The Trust is registered with the Charity Commission for England and Wales as charity number 505756.

Its stated charitable activity is to provide and maintain a playing field and play equipment for use by the general public in Darrington.

Responsibilities

How the Trust is governed

Trustees hold formal responsibility for the charity. Volunteers and community representatives support practical work and events without replacing that accountability.

01

Act for the charity

Trustees must make decisions in the Trust’s best interests and pursue its charitable purpose.

02

Protect resources

The Trust is responsible for using money, property and other resources carefully and appropriately.

03

Maintain oversight

Trustees oversee plans, risks, record-keeping and the work carried out on the charity’s behalf.

04

Report transparently

Required returns and financial information are submitted to the Charity Commission.

Our approach

Simple principles for good governance

Community benefit

Decisions should support the Field’s long-term value to Darrington and its visitors.

Appropriate openness

Public information should be clear and current while personal and security-sensitive details remain protected.

Responsible decisions

Material commitments should be considered, recorded and approved through the Trust’s agreed processes.
