Santa Special is coming back

After our highly successful and enjoyable Santa Special in 2025, we are arranging a date with Santa for his 2026 visit in December—further updates coming soon.

A festive village moment

Making plans with Santa

Santa Special gives children in the village an opportunity to meet Santa in his sleigh and share some Christmas excitement close to home.

The Trust is currently working on the 2026 date and arrangements. We are keeping this page deliberately simple until those details have been agreed, so families can rely on the information published here.

Confirmed so far

Santa is planning a December visit

The precise date, location, booking arrangements and programme have not yet been confirmed.
