Five Mile Run 2026

Everyone’s a winner

The 2026 Darrington Five Mile Run brought 180 runners to the village on Saturday 20 June as part of Feast & Fayre day.

From the start to the finish on the Village Field, the Run added energy, encouragement and a real sense of achievement to the village celebration.

Thank you to everyone who took part, cheered from the route, helped with arrangements or supported the runners on the day.
