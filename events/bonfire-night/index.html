A village tradition

A night that brings people together

Bonfire Night transforms the Village Field into a shared community gathering, with the glow of the bonfire followed by a professional fireworks display.

The event is made possible by people from across the community, and money raised helps the Village Field Trust continue caring for the Field and supporting future community events.

The experience

What Bonfire Night is all about

The central ingredients are simple: a community gathering, a bonfire and a professional display filling the night sky.

01

Community bonfire

A traditional focal point for the evening, prepared through community effort and managed as part of the organised event.

02

Professional fireworks

A colourful display above the Village Field, giving families and neighbours a shared moment to enjoy together.

03

Seasonal refreshments

Previous events have included hot food, drinks and seasonal treats. The confirmed 2026 offer will be published nearer the event.

04

Community powered

Planning, preparation and event-night support all depend on people from the community giving their time.
