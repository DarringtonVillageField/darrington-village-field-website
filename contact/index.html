Help us respond

What to include

Give your message a clear subject and include the relevant event, location or date. If reporting damage or maintenance, describe where it is and attach a photograph when it is safe and appropriate to take one.

Please do not include sensitive personal information unless it is necessary for the Trust to deal with your enquiry.

Field maintenance
Describe the issue and its location as precisely as possible.
Event enquiry
Name the event and the year your question relates to.
Historical material
Explain what you hold and whether the Trust may contact you about copying or publication.

Telephone contact

The Trust is not publishing a general telephone number on this website at present. Please use the appropriate monitored email address above.
