The Field

Your space in the village

Open grass, room to play and a welcoming place to enjoy the outdoors at the heart of Darrington.

Plan your visitReport a problem

Open space

Room for everyday life

The Village Field is a shared green space for the community—a place for informal play, exercise, meeting friends or simply taking a breath outdoors.

It also provides the setting for Darrington’s much-loved annual events, bringing residents and visitors together throughout the year.

Playground

A place for play and adventure

The playground sits alongside the wider green space, giving families room to combine active play with time outdoors.

Children should be supervised by a responsible adult. More detailed equipment and age guidance will be added after the current facilities review.
