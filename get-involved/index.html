Get involved

Small contributions create something special

Give time, share a skill or support an event—there are different ways to help the Village Field and its community flourish.

Tell us how you’d like to help

Ways to help

Find something that suits you

Support does not have to mean a large or permanent commitment. An occasional hour, a useful introduction or a particular skill can all make a difference.

01

Help at an event

Join the team preparing, running or clearing up after one of the village’s community events.

02

Share a skill

Offer practical, creative, professional or organisational experience that could support a project.

03

Support the Field

Help with suitable maintenance activities, community communication or plans for future improvements.

04

Become a sponsor

Support the Trust or an event as a local organisation and help community activity continue.

Community events

Many hands make event day possible

Bonfire Night, Feast & Fayre, the Five Mile Run and Santa Special each depend on people stepping forward.

Roles vary by event and year. They might involve preparation, welcoming visitors, supporting activities, marshalling, practical tasks or helping everything finish tidily.

Ask about event volunteering

Skills and experience

What could you bring?

Community projects benefit from a wide mixture of experience. If you think something you know or do could help, we would be pleased to hear from you.

You might have practical knowledge, event experience, design or photography skills, fundraising ideas, useful local contacts, administrative experience or simply the enthusiasm to join in.

Sponsorship

Support visible community impact

Local businesses and organisations can support the Field, an improvement project or a community event.

Appropriate recognition can be discussed for each opportunity. The Trust will agree the purpose, contribution and acknowledgement before anything is published.

Start a sponsorship conversation

Sponsor information in development

A formal sponsorship pack and approved recognition options will be added when they have been agreed by the Trust.
